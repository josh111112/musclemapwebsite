Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track progressive overload?

MuscleMap automatically calculates your progressive overload. Simply choose between our Double Progression or Standard algorithms, and the app will intelligently suggest your next weight increments based on your performance history.

Is this the best app for tracking muscle recovery?

Yes. Our interactive 3D muscle map visually displays your fatigue levels in real-time. You can customize recovery timelines for each specific muscle group, ensuring you never overtrain or risk injury.

Can I import my workout history?

Absolutely. MuscleMap includes a seamless CSV importer that allows you to bring over all your past workout history from other apps like Strong in seconds, so you never lose your progress.

Do I need an internet connection to log workouts?

No. MuscleMap features robust offline queue syncing. You can log all your sets, reps, and RPEs entirely offline at the gym, and your data will securely back up to the cloud the moment you reconnect.
